About Khan Fokhar

Created out of a deep love for Egyptian artisanal creations, Khan Fokhar is a raw amalgamation of earth and art. Established March 2017, it quickly blossomed into a symbol of raw Egyptian dexterity. Combining an ever-changing kaleidoscope of colors with products literally born out of Egyptian soil, its sole aim is to be able to lend a hand when it comes to strengthening the artisanal community within the land of the Pharaohs. From earth to art; this is Khan Fokhar.
